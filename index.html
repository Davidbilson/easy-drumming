The point is either you are a beginner or intermediate drummer, dedication to practice will increase your potential to be a great drummer, you wanna get better? Know that you have to be determined to work on yourself in these areas;

1. Your state of mind

You have to know that for better experiences, always pay attention to details of how you feel, when bigger drummers play, some feel jealous, some envious, while some appreciate. So you check the state of your mind. When you appreciate other drummers, you begin to practice and play from a good inspiration, and not being boxed up with other feelings. I restricts!!!

2. Know what you want

It is easy to say “ I want to become a great drummer” but do you make commitment towards making It a reality. Set a goal and plan for yourself and follow it daily. You can also make your tutor know your goals as he will help you through the paths.

3. Effective practice and research

great treasures are placed where you have to seek after to improve you skills, don’t just practice but make more research to get the knowledge of how you can practice right, in other not to play dragging beats and rudiments, you play more effortless and natural when you do consistent practice with a good knowledge. Remember great drummers have invested their time in good and knowledgeable practice in the secret. Know that the moment you stop practicing, someone else is doing it, and it can lead to them moving fast ahead.

4. Make right connections

Who you know in your craft really matters in being successful in your area of skill, always remember you cannot do it all alone, do well to get exposed and maintain positive attitude and appearance, because you need to market yourself as valuable to be known. So as you practice, you make fans and meet people to, it helps to expose new skills.
